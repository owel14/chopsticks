How to Play Chopsticks

Objective: Make your opponent's hands both "out."

Setup: Each player starts with one finger up on each hand.

On your turn, you can either:

  • Attack: Tap one of your hands on an opponent's hand to add fingers. If the opponent's hand reaches 5 fingers, that hand is out.
  • Split: Redistribute fingers between your hands.

Winning: You win if your opponent has both hands "out."
